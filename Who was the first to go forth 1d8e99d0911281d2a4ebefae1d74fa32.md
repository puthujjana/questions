# Who was the first to go forth?

Answer in Brief: -
 Category: Vamsa
Sutta References: Mv.1.6.32.1
Answer: The Venerable Koṇḍañña, following his attainment of stream-entry, was the first to go forth by means of the "come monk" (ehi bhikkhu).
